About ConvertPDF

Last updated: March 2026

ConvertPDF is a free, privacy-focused document conversion tool built by a first-year engineering student. Frustrated with online converters that upload files to servers, I decided to build a tool that runs entirely in your browser – no uploads, no data leaks, complete privacy.

All conversions are powered by open-source libraries (pdf-lib, pdf.js, mammoth.js, etc.) and run locally on your device. We don't store any files, and we never will.

This project is open source on GitHub. Contributions and feedback are welcome!
