Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

ConvertPDF is designed with privacy as its core principle. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using ConvertPDF, you agree to the practices described here.

1. No File Uploads – Client‑Side Processing

All document conversions on ConvertPDF happen entirely in your browser. When you select a file, it is read locally using JavaScript and never transmitted to our servers or any third‑party server. This means:

  • We never receive, store, or have access to your documents.
  • Your files remain on your device at all times.
  • There are no file size limits because nothing is uploaded.
  • You can disconnect from the internet after the page loads and most tools will continue to work.

2. Information We Collect

a. Google AdSense

We display ads using Google AdSense, which may use cookies and web beacons to personalize ads based on your visits to this site and other websites. AdSense collects information such as:

  • Your IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages you visit on our site
  • Clickstream data

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings and www.aboutads.info.

b. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. Analytics collects anonymized data such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device type and browser
  • Geographic location (country/city, anonymized)
  • Traffic sources (e.g., search engines, referrals)

This information helps us improve our tools and content. Google Analytics uses cookies; you can learn more about how Google handles data here.

c. Essential Cookies

We may use essential cookies to maintain your preferences (e.g., remembering your consent choices). These cookies do not track you across other sites.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information only for:

  • Showing relevant ads (AdSense)
  • Analyzing site usage to improve user experience (Analytics)
  • Complying with legal obligations

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Third‑Party Services

ConvertPDF uses the following third‑party services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdSense – Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics – Privacy Policy
  • CDN providers (Cloudflare, jsDelivr) – they may log IP addresses for network operations; refer to their respective policies.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Opt‑out of personalized ads: Visit Google Ad Settings.
  • Disable cookies: Adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies. Note that this may affect ad personalization and analytics.
  • Access, correct, or delete your data: Since we do not collect personal data directly, we cannot access or modify it. For data held by Google, refer to their privacy tools.
  • Withdraw consent: You can withdraw consent for cookies at any time via the consent banner (if displayed) or browser settings.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data on our servers. Analytics data is retained by Google according to their retention policies (typically 26 months). AdSense data is retained as described in Google's privacy policy.

7. Children's Privacy

ConvertPDF is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our site, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where Google operates. By using our site, you consent to such transfers. Google adheres to the EU‑US Data Privacy Framework and other transfer mechanisms.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

Our Mission: Your Privacy Comes First

At ConvertPDF, we believe that your documents belong to you—not to some server in the cloud. That's why we built every tool to run entirely in your browser. No uploads, no data mining, no hidden fees. Just fast, reliable conversions that respect your privacy.

Unlike many "free" converters that sell your data or impose file size limits, ConvertPDF gives you unlimited use of all features without ever asking for a sign-up. Our code is open source, so you (or any security researcher) can verify that we truly do what we promise.
