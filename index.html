Why Choose ConvertPDF?

ConvertPDF is a completely free, privacy‑focused document toolkit. Unlike other online converters, your files never leave your device – all conversions happen locally in your browser. This means your sensitive documents stay private and secure.

Whether you're a student converting Markdown notes to PDF, a professional merging multiple PDFs, or a developer generating QR codes, ConvertPDF provides fast, reliable tools without any server uploads.

Our Tools:

  • Markdown → PDF: Perfect for developers and writers. Supports LaTeX math, syntax highlighting, and custom page breaks.
  • DOCX → PDF: Convert Word documents to PDF with perfect formatting – preserves tables, images, and headings.
  • Images → PDF: Combine multiple JPG/PNG images into a single PDF, with auto‑orientation and page size control.
  • PDF Password: Protect your PDFs with encryption. Set permissions for printing, copying, and modifying.
  • Merge PDFs: Combine multiple PDF files into one, with drag‑and‑drop reordering.
  • TXT → DOCX: Convert plain text to formatted Word documents with custom fonts and line spacing.
  • PDF → JPG: Extract images from PDF pages. Choose first page, all pages, or a custom range – download as ZIP.
  • Image → PNG: Convert any image to PNG format with lossless quality.
  • HTML → PDF: Turn HTML snippets into printable PDFs with full CSS support.
  • QR Code Generator: Create custom QR codes with colors, logos, and error correction – download as PNG or SVG.

All tools are 100% free, with no sign‑up required. Your privacy is our priority – no files are ever uploaded to any server.
