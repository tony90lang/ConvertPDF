TXT to DOCX – Turn Plain Text into Polished Word Documents

Plain text files are great for notes and code, but they lack formatting. ConvertPDF’s TXT to DOCX tool adds fonts, line spacing, and even automatic heading detection – all locally, with no uploads.

Features You’ll Love

Choose your style: Pick from four fonts (Arial, Times New Roman, Calibri, Courier New) and sizes from 10pt to 14pt. Line spacing control: Single, 1.15, 1.5, or double. Heading detection: Convert lines starting with # into H1 headings and ## into H2 – perfect for turning outlines into structured documents. Live preview: See the formatted text before you download.

Ideal for students turning notes into essays, writers preparing manuscripts, or developers documenting code. After conversion, you can turn the DOCX into PDF.
