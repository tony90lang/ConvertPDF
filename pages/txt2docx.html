TXT to DOCX Converter

Turn plain text files (.txt) into fully formatted Word documents (.docx). Choose font, size, line spacing, and automatically detect headings – all privately in your browser.

Why Convert TXT to DOCX?

  • Professional formatting: Add fonts, sizes, and line spacing to make your text look polished.
  • Heading detection: Lines starting with # or ## become H1 and H2 headings automatically.
  • Perfect for drafts: Take your rough notes and turn them into shareable documents.
  • Privacy first: Your text stays on your device – no uploads.

How to Use

  1. Upload your .txt file.
  2. Choose your formatting options (font, size, line spacing).
  3. Optionally, check "Detect # headings" to convert markdown‑style headings.
  4. Click "Convert to DOCX" – a preview will appear.
  5. Click "Download DOCX" to save the formatted Word document.

After conversion, you can also turn it into PDF.
