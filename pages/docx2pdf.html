DOCX to PDF Converter

Convert Microsoft Word documents (.docx) to high‑quality PDFs while preserving tables, images, headings, and formatting. Ideal for sharing resumes, reports, and contracts securely – all in your browser.

Why Use This Tool?

  • Formatting intact: Mammoth.js ensures that tables, images, and text styles are carried over.
  • Heading detection: Optionally convert lines starting with # into proper heading tags.
  • Privacy first: Your document never leaves your device – no uploads, no data leaks.
  • Print directly to PDF: Use your browser's built‑in PDF engine for the best results.

How to Convert DOCX to PDF

  1. Upload your Word document using the file picker below.
  2. Choose page size (A4 or Letter) and orientation.
  3. If desired, check "Convert # style headings" to turn markdown‑like headings into actual heading tags.
  4. Click "Print / Save as PDF" and then "Save as PDF" in the print dialog.

After conversion, you can also extract images from the resulting PDF.

Your files stay private – all processing happens locally, so we never see your data.
