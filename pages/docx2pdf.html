DOCX to PDF – Preserve Every Detail, Privately

Microsoft Word documents are the standard for resumes, reports, and contracts. But when you share them, formatting can break – fonts shift, images move, tables collapse. ConvertPDF’s DOCX to PDF tool locks your layout exactly as you designed it, using Mammoth.js to faithfully convert every element.

What Makes It Unique

Perfect formatting: Tables, images, headings, and even complex page margins are preserved. Markdown‑style heading detection: Optionally convert lines starting with # into proper heading tags – ideal if you draft in plain text. Privacy first: Your Word files never leave your browser; no server ever sees them. No upload limits: Convert documents of any size – from a one‑page memo to a 500‑page thesis.

After conversion, take your PDF further – encrypt it or merge it with other files, all client‑side.
