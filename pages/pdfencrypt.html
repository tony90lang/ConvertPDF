PDF Password Protection – Strong Encryption, No Compromises

When you share confidential documents – contracts, financial statements, legal briefs – a simple PDF without a password is a risk. ConvertPDF adds AES‑256 encryption to your PDFs directly in your browser. Your file never touches a server.

Features That Matter

Two‑layer protection: Set an open password to restrict viewing, and a permissions password to control printing, copying, and editing. Real‑time password strength meter: Ensure your password is strong enough. Granular permissions: Allow printing but disallow copying, or block all modifications – you decide. Client‑side only: No one but you ever sees the file or the password.

After encryption, you can still merge the protected PDF or extract images – but only if you have the password.
