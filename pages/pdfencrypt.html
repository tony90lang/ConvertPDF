PDF Password Protector

Add strong AES‑256 encryption to your PDF files with a custom password. Control what users can do – printing, copying, or modifying – while keeping your documents secure.

Why Encrypt PDFs?

  • Confidentiality: Only those with the password can open the file.
  • Permissions: Restrict printing, copying, or editing even after the file is opened.
  • Peace of mind: Ideal for contracts, financial reports, and personal documents.

How It Works

  1. Upload your PDF using the file picker.
  2. Enter a password (and confirm it).
  3. Set permissions: allow or disallow printing, copying, and modifying.
  4. Click "Encrypt & Download" – your protected PDF is ready.

All encryption happens in your browser using the pdf-lib library. Your file never leaves your device.

After encrypting, you may also want to merge it with other files.
