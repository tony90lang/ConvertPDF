Merge PDFs Online

Combine multiple PDF files into one document – quickly, easily, and privately. No uploads, no servers.

Features

  • Combine any number of PDFs: Select multiple files; they will be merged in the order you choose.
  • Reorder pages: Use the up/down buttons to rearrange files before merging.
  • Preserve quality: Pages are copied exactly as they appear in the original files.
  • Private & secure: All processing stays on your device – nothing is uploaded.

How to Merge

  1. Click the file input and select the PDFs you want to combine (hold Ctrl/Cmd for multiple).
  2. If needed, reorder them using the up/down arrows.
  3. Click "Merge & download" – your combined PDF will be generated instantly.

After merging, you can also password‑protect the result.

Your files never leave your browser – absolute privacy.
