Merge PDFs – The Privacy‑First Way to Combine Documents

Combining multiple PDFs is a daily task for many professionals – but most online mergers require uploading your files to a server, exposing sensitive data. ConvertPDF’s Merge tool runs entirely in your browser, using the pdf-lib library to assemble documents locally.

Key Advantages

Unlimited files: Merge as many PDFs as you need – there’s no file count limit because nothing is uploaded. Reorder before merging: Drag‑free up/down buttons let you arrange pages in seconds. Preserved quality: All fonts, images, and annotations stay intact. Instant download: Your merged PDF is ready immediately.

Perfect for compiling quarterly reports, combining research papers, or merging scanned contracts. After merging, you can secure the result with a password.
