QR Code Generator

Create custom QR codes with your own colors, logo, and error correction level. Download as high‑quality PNG or vector SVG – perfect for marketing, business cards, and links.

Features

  • Custom colors: Choose foreground and background colors to match your brand.
  • Logo embedding: Upload a logo to appear in the center of the QR code (error correction must be high enough).
  • Error correction: Select from L, M, Q, H levels for better scanning reliability.
  • Multiple sizes: From small (200px) to print quality (1024px).
  • PNG or SVG: Raster for web, vector for print.

How to Create a QR Code

  1. Enter the text or URL you want to encode.
  2. Adjust size, error correction, and colors as desired.
  3. Optionally upload a logo (square, small images work best).
  4. Click "Generate QR Code" – a preview appears.
  5. Click "Download QR Code" to save as PNG or SVG.

After generating, you can also convert the QR to PNG if needed.

All processing is client‑side – your data stays private.
