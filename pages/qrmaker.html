QR Code Generator – Custom Codes, Full Privacy

From marketing campaigns to contactless menus, QR codes are everywhere. ConvertPDF’s QR generator lets you create custom codes with your brand colors, logos, and error correction – all in your browser, with no data sent to any server.

Why It’s Different

Fully customizable: Choose foreground/background colors, add a central logo, and pick error correction level (L to H). Multiple formats: Download as PNG (web) or SVG (print – infinitely scalable). No size limits: Encode long URLs or text without worrying about server restrictions. Private: The data you encode stays on your device – no third party sees it.

After generating, you can convert the QR to PNG for easier sharing, or embed it in a PDF.
