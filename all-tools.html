Why All Tools Are Free & Private

Every tool on ConvertPDF runs entirely in your browser. Your files never leave your device – no uploads, no servers, no data leaks. We believe essential document tools should be accessible to everyone without compromising privacy.

Unlike other "free" converters that impose file size limits or sell your data, ConvertPDF gives you unlimited use of all features with zero sign-up required. Our code is open source, so you can verify exactly how it works.
